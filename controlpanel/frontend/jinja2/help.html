Home Directory Reset

You need to reset your home directory if any of the following applies:

  • Your RStudio won't start.
  • Your Conda environment is a mess.
  • You need to start RStudio development from a clean state.
  1. From the Analytic tools page, follow the link to reset your home page.
  2. Read the information about what a home reset entails.
  3. Enable the confirmation check-box and click the red "Reset" button.
  4. You'll be returned to the Analytic tools page, but will see a confirmation that the reset is underway.
  5. Depending on the size of your home directory, this process can take between seconds to a couple of minutes. Go make a coffee!
  6. Finally, on the Analytic tools page, click the "Restart" button for RStudio.
  7. Give RStudio a few minutes to start up.
