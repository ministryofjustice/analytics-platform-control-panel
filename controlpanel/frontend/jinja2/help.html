Home Directory Reset

You need to reset your home directory if:

  • Your RStudio won't start.
  • Your Conda environment needs resetting.
  • You need to start RStudio development from a clean state.
  1. From the Analytical tools page, follow the link to reset your home page.
  2. Read the information about what a home reset entails.
  3. Enable the confirmation check-box and click the red "Reset" button.
  4. You will be redirected back to the Analytical tools page, where you will see confirmation that the reset process has started.
  5. Once your environment has been reset, click the "Restart" button for RStudio on the Analytical tools page.
  6. Give RStudio time to start up.
